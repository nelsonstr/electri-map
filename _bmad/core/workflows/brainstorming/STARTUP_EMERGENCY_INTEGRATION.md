# Emergency Services Innovation - Startup Integration Guide

## Overview

This document maps the connection between **startup methodologies/skills** and **emergency services innovation** for the Bmad brainstorming workflow. It provides a bridge between the entrepreneurial domain and the emergency services domain (bombeiros, protecao civil, emergency response).

## Emergency Services Domains

### bombeiros (Fire Service)

**Focus Areas:**
- Fire suppression and rescue operations
- Hazardous materials response
- Technical rescue (water, height, vehicle)
- Emergency medical services integration
- Fire prevention and community risk reduction
- Fire station operations and apparatus management
- Pre-incident planning and inspections
- Wildfire and vegetation fire response

**Key Techniques:** SCAMPER for fireground decisions, Time-Critical Decision Trees, Response Time Decomposition

---

### protecao civil (Civil Protection)

**Focus Areas:**
- Emergency management coordination
- Disaster response and recovery operations
- Evacuation planning and execution
- Shelter operations and management
- Mass casualty incident coordination
- Multi-agency liaison and communication
- Public warning and alert systems
- Community resilience programs
- Special event emergency planning

**Key Techniques:** Multi-Agency Coordination, Joint Operations Protocol, Information Sharing Framework

---

### Emergency Response Flow

**Phases:**
1. **Detection** - 911 call, alarm system, citizen report
2. **Dispatch** - Call processing, resource allocation
3. **Response** - Travel to scene, size-up
4. **Intervention** - Active operations (firefighting, rescue, medical)
5. **Containment** - Fire controlled, rescue complete
6. **Recovery** - Scene clearance, investigation, restoration

**Key Techniques:** Response Time Decomposition, Time-Critical Decision Tree, Resource Flow Analysis
